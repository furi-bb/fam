Weekly Recurring Group Activities
Minigame/Group Activity Saturdays
  • The minigame or activity will be polled in our Discord server #events channel and decided ahead of time.
  • Time of the group activity may vary. This will also be polled and announced.
  • If you have a suggestion for a group activity we could do together, please feel free to reach out to our leadership team.
  • Current activity list rotation may include: Tempoross, Guardians of the Rift, Wintertodt, Mastering Mixology, Giants' Foundry, Zalcano, Soul Wars, Forestry, Pest Control, Penguin Agility Course, Pyramid Plunder, and Volcanic Mine.
Raids & PvM Sundays
  • The raid or PvM activity will be polled in our Discord server #events channel and decided ahead of time.
  • Time of the group activity may vary. This will also be polled and announced.
  • If you have a suggestion for a group activity we could do together, please feel free to reach out to our leadership team.
  • Current raids/PvM activity list rotation may include: Chambers of Xeric, Theatre of Blood, Tombs of Amascut, Barrows, Perilous Moons, GWD Bosses, Barbarian Assault, Hueycoatl, and Corporeal Beast.
Monthly Competitions
Skill of the Month
  • The skill to be measured will be polled in our Discord server #events channel and decided ahead of time.
  • The competition will last a month and clan members' XP will be tracked through TempleOSRS.
  • If you have a suggestion for a skill competition, please feel free to reach out to our leadership team.
  • Winner of the competition will receive an exclusive in-game clan rank icon for the duration of the following month after event ends.

Skill of the Month (May 2025): Slayer
Boss of the Month
  • The boss to kill will be polled in our Discord server #events channel and decided ahead of time.
  • The competition will last a month and clan members' boss kill count will be tracked through TempleOSRS.
  • If you have a suggestion for a boss we should feature, please feel free to reach out to our leadership team.
  • Winner of the competition will receive an exclusive in-game clan rank icon for the duration of the following month after event ends.

Boss of the Month (May 2025): Moons of Peril
