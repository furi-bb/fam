Welcome to Clan Fam!


We are a budding OSRS community of friends. Everyone with an open mind and respect for others is welcome here!


You must be over 18 years old to be a part of Clan Fam.


Violating the following rules will result in disciplinary action (a warning, a suspension, a kick, or a ban.) You must read and agree to the following rules before you can participate in Clan Fam:


You agree to:

1.
Common Decency:
Always be courteous of others. Treat them as you would like to be treated.
2.
No Hate Speech:
Do not engage in any homophobia, transphobia, misogyny, racism, or any other kind of hate speech, even in a joking manner. Violating this rule will result in an immediate ban from the clan.
3.
No Sexually Explicit Content
Please refrain from posting explicit content that involves sexual themes or nudity.
4.
Discord Server Profile Naming Convention
Your name in our clan's discord server must match your OSRS Handle. If you would like to use a preferred name, put your handle in parentheses next to your name, as such: Preferred name (OSRS handle)
5.
No Malicious or Overtly Suspicious Activity:
Never scam or trick others into giving you something, regardless of whether you are dealing with clannies or non-clan members. See rule #1, "Clan Bossing/Group Activities Etiquette", and the "Lending Gear/Items Warning" posts.
