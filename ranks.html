All active members of Clan Fam may advance their in-game rank/role assignment as well as earn the corresponding Discord Server roles by consistently following our rules of conduct, maintaining their presence in our Discord server, and participating in clan events from time to time.


You do not need to participate in every group event or gathering to be eligible.


You will be ineligible to advance your rank in Clan Fam if you leave our Discord server, even if you stay in the clan in-game. You may request the admins to reinstate your rank if you decide to rejoin at any time.

Opal
Requirements
  • You agree to Clan Fam's rules of conduct as listed on our website and Discord server
  • You applied to Clan Fam on our Discord server and received approval by an admin member
  • Neither your in-game player name nor your Discord handle/profile picture can be considered obscene or hateful
  • You are not on a RuneWatch scammer list
Permissions
  • You may join and speak in our in-game clan chat "Fam"
  • You may enter our clan hall located south-east of the Grand Exchange on World 322
  • You may receive drops from party balloons in Clan Hall
  • You may participate in our non-moderator text and voice channels in Discord
Jade
Requirements
  • You have been a member of Clan Fam for more than a month
  • You are present in our Discord server (If you leave the Discord server you will not be able to advance to a rank above Opal.)
  • You have participated in the clan chat some time within the past month
  • All the requirements listed for lower ranks apply to this rank as well
Permissions
  • The permissions for Jade rank are the same as those for Opal
  • You may create private threads on our Discord server
Red Topaz
Requirements
  • You have been a member of Clan Fam for six months or longer
  • All the requirements listed for lower ranks apply to this rank as well
  • You have participated in at least one clan event within the past six months
Permissions
  • The permissions for Red Topaz rank are the same as those for Jade and Opal
  • You may mention @everyone, @here, and all roles in our Discord server
Sapphire
Requirements
  • You have been a member of Clan Fam for nine months or longer
  • All the requirements listed for lower ranks apply to this rank as well
Permissions
  • The permissions for Sapphire rank are the same as those for Red Topaz, Jade, and Opal
  • You may create polls in our Discord server
Emerald
Requirements
  • You have been a member of Clan Fam for a year or longer
  • All the requirements listed for lower ranks apply to this rank as well
Permissions
  • The permissions for Emerald rank are the same as those for Sapphire, Red Topaz, Jade, and Opal
Ruby
Requirements
  • You have been a member of Clan Fam for eighteen months or longer
  • All the requirements listed for lower ranks apply to this rank as well
Permissions
  • The permissions for Ruby rank are the same as those for Sapphire, Red Topaz, Jade, and Opal
  • You may apply for a Clan Fam leadership role; starting with the Diamond rank (Diamond, Dragonstone, Onyx, and Zenyte are leadership roles.)
Diamond (Leadership Role)
Requirements
  • You have posted an application for Diamond rank leadership role in our Discord server and received approval from an admin member
  • All the requirements listed for lower ranks apply to this rank as well
  • You are active in the Discord, the in-game clan chat, and uphold the values we hold dear as a community
